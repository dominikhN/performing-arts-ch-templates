About

The SAPA Foundation collects, documents, archives and disseminates works from the field of the performing arts (dance, theatre, performance art, etc.) produced in Switzerland, associated with Switzerland or important to it. All these documents form the Helvetica of the performing arts. Once completed, performing-arts.ch will provide information on the archival holdings of SAPA and on performing arts in Switzerland as Linked Data.

This preview version of the database contains only a small part of our data. Primarily, it can be helpful to identify persons, groups and venues. The database also contains information about performing arts productions. However, this is not yet fully linked and thus only partially searchable. Further data and media files will be added later. Meanwhile, the archival holdings of the former Swiss Dance Collection in Lausanne and Zurich are accessible at catalogue.collectiondeladanse.ch/tanzarchiv/.

A technical documentation of the data is available at sapa.github.io/spa-specifications/.

If you have any questions or feedback, please contact birk.weiberg@sapa.swiss.
